Privacy Policy

App: Forest Puzzle
Developer: Son Software App
Last updated: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Forest Puzzle ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Forest Puzzle does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, location, etc.) to our servers.

All game progress (current level, hint count, scores, settings) is stored locally on your device using the device's built-in storage and is never shared with us.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Forest Puzzle displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data to deliver personalised or non-personalised ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, Android Advertising ID on Android)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • IP address (used to infer approximate location)
  • App usage data

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. For details on how Google uses this data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from AdMob

3. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14 and later, Forest Puzzle requests permission to track your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) before showing personalised ads. If you deny this permission, you will still see ads but they will not be personalised to your interests. You can change this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

4. Children's Privacy

Forest Puzzle is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove that information.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete. Game save data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.
